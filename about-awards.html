About the T2W Members' Choice Awards

Trade2Win Members’ Choice Awards have been dishing out awards since 2003 to the best of the best within the financial industry. We’ve kept a list of all our winners so you can look back into the past.

History of the Awards

The MCAs first launched on T2W back in 2003, since then they have gained industry recognition as the de facto trading community awards. In 2007 we launched this dedicated website, www.tradingawards.com, to give the MCAs a permanent home accessible throughout the year.

Judging Panel

The judging panel for the MCAs are the members of Trade2Win (T2W), the world's largest trading community. Over 247,000 traders (37,000 more than last year!) have registered with T2W since it launched 11 years ago, making the MCAs possibly the most democratic and representative trading awards on the planet!

Venue and Winners

The MCAs has no actual venue hosting the awards ceremony, we announce the winners here on TradingAwards.com and notify them by email/phone.
